About these notes

Started in 2018 by Thierry Graff.
There are notes until part I, chapter 7.2 ; for the moment, I stopped watching the videos (because I bought the book).
You can download these notes from the git repository : gitea.larzac.info/tig12/categories.

The notes are written in english but I use french conventions for capitalization and spaces.
- The characters ; : ? ! are preceeded and followed by a white space.
- Names of months, weekdays, languages etc. are not capitalized.
